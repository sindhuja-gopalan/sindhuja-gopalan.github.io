Sindhuja Gopalan!

I work in the area of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning for more than 9 years. I am a Senior Data Scientist with Uniphore Software Systems, Chennai, India. Previously, I worked as Reserach Engineer in AU-KBC Research Centre, Madras Institute of Technology, Chennai. I did my Ph.D under the guidance of Dr. Sobha Lalitha Devi from Anna University, thesis titled "Identification of Discourse Relations from Biomedical Texts and its Application in Cause-Effect Extraction".
